Booking & Reservations

How do I book a car?

To book a car, simply browse our available vehicles, select your preferred car, choose your rental dates and time, and complete the booking process. You can book online through our website or mobile app.

What documents do I need to rent a car?

You'll need a valid driving license, government-issued ID proof (Aadhar card, PAN card, or passport), and a credit/debit card for payment. For international travelers, an international driving permit may be required.

Can I modify my booking after confirmation?

Yes, you can modify your booking up to 24 hours before the rental start time. Changes are subject to availability and may incur additional charges.

Payment & Billing

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, UPI payments, net banking, and digital wallets like Paytm, Google Pay, and PhonePe.

When is the payment charged?

Payment is charged at the time of booking confirmation. A security deposit may be held and will be refunded after the rental period.

Are there any hidden charges?

No hidden charges. All fees are clearly displayed during the booking process. Additional charges may apply for fuel, tolls, or damages.

Cancellation & Refunds

What is your cancellation policy?

Free cancellation up to 24 hours before rental start time. Cancellations within 24 hours may incur a cancellation fee of 50% of the rental amount.

How long does it take to process refunds?

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days and will be credited to your original payment method.

Insurance & Safety

What insurance coverage is included?

Basic insurance coverage is included with all rentals. This covers third-party liability and basic damage protection. Additional coverage options are available.

What happens in case of an accident?

In case of an accident, immediately contact our 24/7 support team. Document the incident with photos and police report if required. Our insurance team will guide you through the process.

Technical Support

The website is not loading properly

Try clearing your browser cache, updating your browser, or using a different browser. If the issue persists, contact our technical support team.

I can't complete my booking

Check your internet connection and ensure all required fields are filled. If the problem continues, try refreshing the page or contact our support team.

General Questions

What are your operating hours?

Our customer support is available 24/7. Car pickup and drop-off services are available from 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM daily.

Do you offer airport pickup/drop?

Yes, we offer airport pickup and drop services at major airports in Madhya Pradesh. Please book this service in advance.
